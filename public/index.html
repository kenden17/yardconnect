Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need an account to post a task?

No. Anyone can post a task without creating an account. You'll just need to provide your name, contact info, and a government ID for verification.

How does payment work?

You pay through the platform before work begins. Funds are held securely and released to the student only after you confirm the work is done. No cash, no Venmo — everything goes through Stripe.

Who are the students?

All students are between 16 and 24 years old and verified with a school or k12 email address. They must agree to community guidelines before joining.

What if the work isn't done right?

You control when payment is released. If you're not satisfied, don't release payment and contact us at support@campushands.app. We'll help mediate.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. Your home address is never shown publicly — only the task's city and ZIP are visible. Full addresses are only revealed to the student you specifically hire.

What jobs are not allowed?

Electrical, plumbing, roofing, chainsaw use, anything involving weapons, hazardous chemicals, medical care, or illegal activity. See our full list in the Community Guidelines.

How old do students have to be for babysitting?

Workers must be 18 or older to apply for babysitting and childcare jobs. The platform enforces this automatically.

How much does Campus Hands charge?

Posting a task is completely free. Campus Hands takes a 5% platform fee from the student's payment. There are no hidden fees for posters.
