How It Works

Simple as 1 – 2 – 3

For Students
1

Sign Up with School Email

Register using your k12 or school email. We verify your address to keep the platform safe.

2

Browse & Apply

Find jobs near you — mowing, raking, hauling, cleaning. Apply with a short message.

3

Do the Work. Get Paid.

Complete the job. The homeowner releases payment directly to you via Stripe.

For Homeowners
1

Post a Job

Describe what you need done, set a fair pay amount, and publish your listing.

2

Review Applicants

See who applied and pick the right student for the job. All students are school-verified.

3

Pay Securely

Once the job is done, release payment through our secure Stripe integration.
