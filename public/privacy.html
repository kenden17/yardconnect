Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026  ·  Applies to all Campus Hands users.

This Privacy Policy explains how Campus Hands ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information about you when you use the Campus Hands platform ("Platform"). By using the Platform, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

We take the privacy of minors seriously. If you have concerns about a minor's data, please contact us immediately at privacy@campushands.app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Student Workers provide:

  • Full name
  • School or educational email address
  • Date of birth
  • Password (stored as a one-way hash — never readable)

Task Posters provide:

  • Full legal name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Home address
  • Date of birth
  • Government-issued ID type and ID number
  • A photo of their government-issued ID
  • Task address (kept private from public listing)

All users may also provide:

  • Messages sent through the platform
  • Ratings and written reviews
  • Task descriptions and job details

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and browser/device type (via server logs)
  • Pages visited and actions taken on the Platform
  • Session cookies used to keep you logged in

1.3 Payment Information

Campus Hands does not store credit card numbers or full payment details. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. When you make a payment, your card data goes directly to Stripe and is subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy. Campus Hands only receives a transaction confirmation and a payment intent ID.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the Platform: matching students with tasks, processing payments, sending notifications.
  • To verify identity: government ID information is collected to deter bad actors, comply with our safety obligations, and facilitate law enforcement requests if an incident involving a minor is reported.
  • To enforce our Terms: detecting fraud, abuse, prohibited tasks, and community guideline violations.
  • To communicate with you: transaction confirmations, assignment notifications, support responses.
  • To improve the Platform: understanding how users interact with the site (aggregated and anonymized where possible).

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Between Users — Limited Disclosure

  • Task addresses are never shown publicly. They are only shared with the specific Student Worker who is accepted for a task.
  • Poster phone numbers are not displayed on the Platform. Contact should happen through the task coordination process.
  • Student email addresses are visible to the Poster of a task they applied to, for coordination purposes only.
  • Government ID information (name, ID number, photo) is only accessible to Campus Hands administrators. It is never shown to other users.

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the Platform, including:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Railway / hosting provider — server infrastructure
  • Email provider — transactional emails (e.g., task assignment notifications)

These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only to provide services to us and not for their own purposes.

3.3 Law Enforcement and Legal Obligations

We will disclose user information, including government ID photos and personal details, to law enforcement or legal authorities when:

  • We receive a valid subpoena, court order, or legal process;
  • A safety incident involving a minor is reported to us;
  • We believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to prevent imminent harm to any person; or
  • Required to comply with applicable law.

By posting a task on Campus Hands, you explicitly consent to this potential disclosure.

3.4 Business Transfers

If Campus Hands is acquired, merged, or its assets are sold, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections described here.

4. Data Retention

  • Student accounts: retained while the account is active. Deleted within 90 days of a verified deletion request, unless required for legal purposes.
  • Task and transaction records: retained for a minimum of 3 years for legal and safety purposes.
  • Government ID photos and poster identity data: retained for a minimum of 3 years from the date of task posting, regardless of account deletion. This is required for legal and safety compliance.
  • Ratings and reviews: may be retained indefinitely in anonymized form.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • JWT-based authentication with secure, HTTP-only cookies
  • ID photo files stored in a restricted directory accessible only to authenticated administrators
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users in accordance with applicable law.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Campus Hands allows users who are 16 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at privacy@campushands.app and we will promptly remove the account and associated data.

For users between 16 and 17 years old, parents or guardians may request access to or deletion of their child's account data by contacting us at the same email address.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements (see Section 4).
  • Portability: You may request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out of communications: You may opt out of non-essential emails at any time by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@campushands.app. We will respond within 30 days. Note that certain data (particularly government ID information submitted as part of a task posting) cannot be deleted due to legal safety obligations.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do not sell personal information).
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@campushands.app with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Campus Hands uses:

  • Session cookies — HTTP-only, secure cookies used solely to maintain your login session. These are necessary for the Platform to function.
  • localStorage — used in the browser to store your session token for API requests.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics platforms, or advertising cookies.

10. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Stripe). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing them with any personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Platform after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@campushands.app
  • General support: support@campushands.app
